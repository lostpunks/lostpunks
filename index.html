Lost Punk Society

Welcome to the Lost Punk Society, where the descendants of the original CryptoPunks gather to share their colorful stories. New members can only be added by those who own both a father punk and a mother punk. But beware! No punk can ever have more than two children, and parents cannot be closely related.

Will siblings go their separate ways to be reunited years later? How often will twins occur? Will we see any lost aliens or apes? Do we risk a zombie infestation? Only time will tell...
